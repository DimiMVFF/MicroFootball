The Micronational Virtual Football Federation., commonly known by the acronym MVFF, is an international Virtual football body for micronations Its headquarters are located in Niš, and its current president is Dimitrije Golubović, who took the presidency on March 28, 2017, as he is one of the founders. The MVFF is responsible for the organisation and governance of football's major virtual micronational tournaments, most notably the MVFF World Cup. Matches are generated in TSS and PES.The organisation is divided in two organizations..The Asia-Oceania confederation, European confederation of virtual football and The American Federation. The World Cup will feature 8 teams.
