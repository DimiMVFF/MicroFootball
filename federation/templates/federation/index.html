The Micronational Virtual Football Federation., commonly known by the acronym MVFF, is an international Virtual football body for micronations Its headquarters are located in Brighouse, and its current president is Edwin Farrar, who took the presidency on February 28, 2017, as he is the founder. The MVFF is responsible for the organisation and governance of football's major virtual micronational tournaments, most notably the MVFF World Cup. Matches will be generated here. The organisation will be split into three organisations.The Asia-Oceania confederation, European confederation of virtual football and The American Federation. The world cup will feature 12 teams, with each confederation having 4 slots each. Qualifying tournaments in each confederation will decide the qualifiers, with the semi-finalists qualifying for the world cup.
